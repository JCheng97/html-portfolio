The Best Movie According to Jun

My top 3 movies of the all-time.

The Pursuit of Happyness

A single father (Will Smith) struggles to carve out a better life for himself and his son.

Green Book

Two men confront racism and danger while taking a road trip through the Deep South in 1962.Must watch!

The Wolf of Wall Street

New York stockbroker Jordan Belfort (Leonardo DiCaprio) develops habits of excess and corruption.
